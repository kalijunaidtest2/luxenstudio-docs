Mip-Luxen#

A Multiscale Representation for Anti-Aliasing Neural Radiance Fields

Paper Website

Running Model#

ns-train mipluxen

Overview#

The primary modification in MipLuxen is in the encoding for the field representation. With the modification the same mip-Luxen field can be use for the coarse and fine steps of the rendering hierarchy.

In the field, the Positional Encoding (PE) is replaced with an Integrated Positional Encoding (IPE) that takes into account the size of the sample.
